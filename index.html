Reshaping the way individuals find their next destination
Wander is a mobile travel platform designed to help users discover authentic, local experiences beyond typical tourist attractions.

By aggregating hidden gems from niche sources and tailoring recommendations based on user preferences and location, Wander encourages spontaneous exploration while supporting local communities.
THE PROBLEM →
Addressing homogeneity in digital spaces to make experiences feel more unique, engaging, and exploratory
Homogenized digital experiences. Platforms prioritize popular and familiar content, making online spaces feel repetitive and limiting users' ability to discover unique or diverse options.

Decline in authentic discovery. Algorithm-driven trends and mass appeal overshadow niche content, making it harder for users to find genuine, local, or unconventional experiences.

Impact on real-world exploration. Digital conformity influences real-world behaviour—shaping where people go and what they value—ultimately reducing spontaneity, individuality, and meaningful exploration.
SOLUTION →
Inspiring exploration through crafted travel experiences

Rewiring the way companies and their employees work remotely
Overlap is a productivity platform designed for remote startups, helping teams collaborate across different schedules and time zones. It introduces "overlap hours" to show when teammates are available at the same time and includes a "Catch-up" feature that summarizes missed meetings.

Beyond productivity, Overlap supports team connection. Randomized "Hangouts" encourage spontaneous interactions, while "Rooms" offer shared spaces for real-time collaboration with tools like whiteboards.
THE PROBLEM →
Hybrid and remote work bring flexibility, but also miscommunication, lost progress, and weaker team connections.
Homogenized digital experiences. Platforms prioritize popular and familiar content, making online spaces feel repetitive and limiting users' ability to discover unique or diverse options.

SOLUTION →
Allowing seamless team communication for hybrid and remote work while boosting team morale and productivity between employees

UW/UX: Expanding the product design & UX community

UW/UX is a dynamic student-led initiative dedicated to fostering the UX and product design community at the University of Waterloo. They welcome individuals from both design and non-design backgrounds by organizing engaging workshops, speaker conferences, and designathons.

As a design lead, I design and publish compelling assets that enhance our community presence. My role involves crafting engaging illustrations for UX-related content, refining and expanding our brand identity, and analyzing social media growth to optimize our visual storytelling.

I'm fortunate to have the support of fellow UW/UX creatives, allowing us to bring fresh, impactful designs to life.
